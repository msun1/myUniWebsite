What is an internship?

According to Wikipedia, an internship is a period of work experience offered by an organization or company for a limited period of time. Once confined to medical graduates, internship is used for a wide range of placements in businesses, non-profit organizations and government agencies.

Requirments?

Are enrolled in a degree program at the time of the internship

Have completed their Foundation year (at least 27 credits)

Have a cumulative GPA of 2.5 or above

SOURCE: https://www.mica.edu/career-development/mica-internship-program/internship-eligibility/
